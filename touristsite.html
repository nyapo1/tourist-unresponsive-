Blue Night Skies

The key draw of the Blue Night Skies is the exceptional quality and clarity of the night sky. Thanks to the area's minimal light pollution and high elevation, stargazers are treated to an awe-inspiring display of twinkling celestial bodies. On clear nights, the Milky Way galaxy stretches across the sky in a breathtaking panorama, and visitors can spot countless stars, planets, and even distant galaxies with the naked eye.

Diani Beach

Diani Beach is a renowned coastal destination renowned for its pristine white sand and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Nestled along the Indian Ocean, this picturesque beach offers an idyllic setting for swimming, sunbathing,and water sports such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and sailing. Nearby, visitors can explore charming seaside towns, indulge in fresh seafood, and discover local Swahili culture and traditions.
Diani Beach

International Archives

The International Archives is a captivating hub for history, culture, and research. This expansive facility houses an extensive collection of artifacts, documents, and materials chronicling the rich heritage of the region and its people.
International Archives

Abedare Forest

The Abedare Forest is a lush, biodiverse haven teeming with rare flora and fauna. This protected conservation area is home to majestic trees, cascading waterfalls, and a wide array of animal species, including endangered primates and large predators.
Abedare Forest

Kwachu Memorial Hills

The Kwachu Memorial Hills are a picturesque natural attraction located in the heart of the countryside. Featuring rolling green hills, serene valleys, and panoramic viewpoints, the Kwachu Memorial Hills offer visitors a peaceful escape from the bustling urban centers.
